About MR Price

Mr Price Group Limited is a South African retail firm that is publicly traded. Mr Price launched in 1987 after the owners purchased a controlling interest in John Orrs Holdings, which at the time had two trading divisions: The Hub and Miladys. Mr Price, Miladys, Mr Price Sport, Mr Price Home, and Sheet Street are their current trading divisions.
