About Incredible Connection

Incredible Connection is a South African retailer of consumer goods and information technology. Michael Glezerson and Michael David Smith launched Software Connection in 1989. (Australia). The first store opened in Johannesburg's Sandton City. The company is presently South Africa's and Africa's largest consumer electronics and information technology store. Incredible Connection was purchased by Connection Group Holdings Limited on December 15, 1998, and now operates as a subsidiary of that firm.
