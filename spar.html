About Spar

SPAR, formerly DESPAR, is a worldwide Dutch food retail franchise that runs independently owned and operated locations. Adriaan van Well started it in the Netherlands in 1932, and it today has over 13,320 outlets in 48 countries. The company's headquarters are in Amsterdam. The company has a cooperation program and is present in the majority of European countries, as well as a number of others in Asia, Africa, and Oceania. Spar was created in the Dutch town of Zegwaart in 1932. In 1953, an International Spar office was established in Amsterdam to oversee and promote the company across Europe and other continents. Spar is one of the most trusted supermarkets in South Africa providing great deals to all their customers to meet their needs.
