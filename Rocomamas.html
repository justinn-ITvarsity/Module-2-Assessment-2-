About Rocomamas

RocoMamas, which opened in July 2013, has quickly become one of South Africa's most popular restaurants, with a devoted following of burger fans. The creative décor, rock ‘n roll feel, distinctive menu options, and open-style kitchen so visitors can see (and smell) the food being produced reflect their brand position of “Not Normal.” With RocoMamas' growth in the future, Spur Corporation is looking forward to maintaining the brand's basic characteristics and distinctiveness, which customers unquestionably enjoy. RocoMamas' winning recipe for success in South Africa is a combination of unusual menu items, a modern dining ambience, and a bill that won't break the bank.
