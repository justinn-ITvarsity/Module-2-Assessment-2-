About Pick N Pay

The Pick n Pay Group is a retailer that specializes on fast-moving consumer items. Pick n Pay, Boxer, and TM Supermarkets are the three names under which the company operates. Pick n Pay also runs one of Sub-Saharan Africa's major online grocery businesses. As of 2018, the corporation had over 1,600 outlets spread across seven Southern African countries. The corporation revealed a planned expansion into Nigeria in that year's annual report, as well as the possibility of extending into Ghana. In South Africa and Swaziland, Pick n Pay also operates outlets under the Boxer brand.
