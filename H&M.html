About H&M

H&M is a worldwide apparel retailer based in Sweden that specializes in fast-fashion clothing. H&M operates in 74 countries, with over 5,000 stores under multiple business brands and 126,000 full-time equivalent employees as of November 2019. It is the world's second-largest clothes store, after Spain's Inditex. Erling Persson founded the company, which is now run by his son Stefan Persson and Helena Helmersson. H&M offered collaborations with Karl Lagerfeld, Stella McCartney, Madonna and many more. The company allows for online shopping in more than 33 countries.
